Captain America: Civil War (2016)

    Overview

    Genres: Action/Adventure/Sci-Fi
    Duration: 2h 27min
    Country: USA | Germany
    Director: Anthony Russo, Joe Russo
    Box office: 1.132 billion$
    Budget: 250,000,000$
    initial release: 6 May 2016
    Rating : 7.8/10

    Storyline

    With many people fearing the actions of super heroes, the government
    decides to push for the Hero Registration Act, a law that limits a hero's
    actions. This results in a division in The Avengers. Iron Man stands with
    this Act, claiming that their actions must be kept in check otherwise citi-
    es will continue to be destroyed, but Captain America feels that saving
    the world is daring enough and that they cannot rely on the government
    to protect the world. This escalates into an all-out war between Team Iron
    Man (Iron Man, Black Panther, Vision, Black Widow, War Machine, and
    Spider-Man) and Team Captain America (Captain America, Bucky Barnes,
    Falcon, Scarlet Witch, Hawkeye, and Ant Man) while a new villain emerges.
